RateYourPlate Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the RateYourPlate mobile application (the "Service") operated by Ryan Chakov ("us", "we", or "our").

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information or data from users of the RateYourPlate mobile application. All data generated and stored by the app remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Usage Data

We may collect non-personal information that your device sends whenever you use our Service ("Usage Data"). This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), device type, device version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

Use of Data

We use the collected Usage Data for various purposes:

  • To monitor and analyze the usage of the Service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal information or Usage Data collected by the Service. All data generated and stored by the app remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Location Data

The RateYourPlate app may utilize location data provided by Apple Maps for specific features such as searching for nearby restaurants or viewing restaurant locations on a map. However, we do not collect, store, or transmit any location data from users.
